Biography

I am now a 3rd year M.S candidate in both Broadband Network & Digital Media Lab and Shenzhen Key Lab of Broadband Network and Multimedia at Tsinghua University under the supervision of Prof. Yongbing Zhang. I received B.Eng. degree from College of Electronic Information and Automation, Tianjin University of Science & Technology, Tianjin, China, in 2014. My current research interests include: Computational Imaging, Fourier ptychography and Optical Bioimaging.
